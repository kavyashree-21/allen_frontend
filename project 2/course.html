Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Your journey to academic excellence begins with ALLEN's trusted guidance and support.

NEET

ALLEN Career Institute is one of India’s leading coaching institutes for medical and engineering entrance exams. It plays a major role in helping aspirants prepare for the NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test). On its website, ALLEN provides detailed and organized information about the NEET exam to guide students and parents. We offer both offline and online courses.

JEE

ALLEN Career Institute is a trusted name in coaching for competitive exams like JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) — the gateway to top engineering colleges like the IITs and NITs. The institute provides complete information and guidance for JEE aspirants through its website and digital platforms. JEE Mains along with JEE Advanced coaching will be provided. We offer both offline and online courses.

GATE

GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) is a national-level exam conducted for admission to postgraduate engineering programs (like M.Tech, ME) and recruitment into PSUs (Public Sector Undertakings). Though ALLEN is famous for NEET and JEE, institutes that offer GATE coaching typically share the following types of information on their websites.
